Make me Pretty!

Your job for this lab is to make this page clean, nice-looking and professional. Do this by adding a Cascading Style Sheet. You can use an internal or external style sheet. Do not change the text on the page, and be sure to include all of the links and pictures. You can, of course, make changes to the HTML to refer to classes defined in your style sheet, resize the images in the HTML, etc. You cannot embed formatting commands directly into this document unless they are in the internal style sheet.

Web Design

There are many web pages out there that discuss both good and bad design. According to Ratz.com, some Good Design Features are:

  • Background does not interrupt the text
  • Text is big enough to read, but not too big
  • Navigation is consistent throoughout the site
  • Link colors coordinate with page colors
  • Buttons are not big and dorky
  • Every graphic has an alt label
  • Every graphic link has a matching text link
  • Graphics and backgrounds use browser-safe colors
  • Pages download quickly

A fun page to check for what not to do is Web Pages that Suck
